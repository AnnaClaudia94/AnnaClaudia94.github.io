The 'Irish Affair'

This cocktail is a dreamy mixture of white rum, Baileys and white creme de cacao, with an optional sprig of mint for a cheeky kick.

Niamh O'Connor

The 'Manhattan Heights'

This feisty concoction of gin, cointreau and grapefruit juice will ensure you have a time you shouldn't forget - but might not remember!

Louisa Ambrose

The 'Sweet Sophistication'

This delicious cocktail is a combination of crushed almond, coconut and raspberry, with a hint of gin and prosecco to add some lift.

Milly Johnson
